Our Mission

            Agriculture supply chain has been plagued by inefficiencies, lack of transparency
            and complex intermediaries, which often leads to higher cost for consumers and 
            lower returns for farmers. Our mission is to propose a software platform which aimed
            to bridging the gap between farmers consumers and retailers.
            This platform will enable direct communication between consumers, farmers and allow 
            farmers to reach a broader market, consumers to access fresh produce at competitive 
            prices and retailers to streamline procurement process.
